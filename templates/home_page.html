How It Works?

1. Login or Create an account if you haven't already.

2. Click on products to go to products page.

3. Enter quantity of items that you want to purchase and add them to cart.

4. Click on My Cart Icon on top right corner to view your cart and click "proceed to buy".

5. Confirm your order and you will be given an ORDER_ID.

6. Show this ORDER_ID in LaFresco Store to get your items.
